About us.

        Hello! We are PPP, otherwise known as Prince's Pamper Palace.  Established right here in South Brunswick, this is the only pet store that actually treats your pets as our own, giving each and every one their royal treatment. Why should you come to our store then? We give great deals on our services (found in the STORE tab on the top-right corner), we use non-irritating soap when washing your dogs, tools that don't hurt your pets, and most importantly, we have a small outdoor area that is free for all our dogs to play around on after their service.
