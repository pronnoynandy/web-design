Technology

Audi quattro

Four is superior to two.

Thirty-five years ago, Audi quattro became the first permanent all-wheel drive system designed for passenger vehicles - and it's been the front-runner ever since. Today quattro plays an essential role in the Audi experience, ensuring its place as an engineering legend.


Audi e-tron

Take charge of your drive.

The Audi e-tron is the plug-in electric hybrid that delivers a whole new driving experience, with exhilarating performance and no worries about range limitations.


Interior design

Redefining comfort and style.

Audi engineers and designers not only challenge convention, they pioneer the ways you connect with your Audi. Everything is explored, from the overall design aesthetic down to the tactile feel of every button, even the way they sound. That precise level of detail is applied to every element throughout the cabin and is a benchmark of refinement you've come to expect.


Audi MMI

Think of this as built-in intuition.

The Audi MMI operating system allows you to instinctively manage an array of systems, including navigation, entertainment, interior, and even ride dynamics on models with Audi drive select. The result is a balance of simplicity, intuitiveness, and elegance to enhance your ownership experience.


Exterior design

Some call it obsessive.

We admit that our designers' compulsive dedication to uniting art and engineering is unrivaled. With the arresting lines of our signature Audi Singleframe grille, the distinctive LED daytime running lights, and the intimidating, visible engine compartment of the R8, you may become a little obsessed with Audi exterior design yourself.


Audi connect

Drive smarter.

Audi connect with Google Earth satellite imagery is your guide to places of interest, travel information, weather reports, fuel prices, and more. You can even send destinations with your myAudi account on your desktop straight to your car.


Audi ultra

Can less be more?

Audi ultra lightweight technology uses advanced material and construction methods that allow our cars to be lighter. Lightweight design means the minimum material in the right place for the best performance. This is proof that less is definitely more.
